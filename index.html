Research

Areas of philosophy I'm interested in include ethics but also the philosophy of mind and language.

In ethics, I'm interested in puzzles in population ethics. What are our obligations to future generations? What are the correct principles of the aggregation of well-being?

In the philosophy of language, my interests lie where questions of meaning and questions of mental content connect. How do linguistic and psychological intentionality relate?
